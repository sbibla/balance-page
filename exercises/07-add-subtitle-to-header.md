# Exercise 7: Add a Personal Subtitle to the Header

**Level:** Beginner
**Skills practiced:** Adding a new element in HTML

---

## Your Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude:

> Below the line that says "Built with Claude Code" in the header, add a new line with your job title or a fun one-sentence description of yourself.

---

## What to look for when done

- A new line of text appears below "Built with Claude Code" in the header
- It shows your description
- The header doesn't look broken

---

*Tip: This teaches you that you can add new content by just describing what you want and where it should go.*
