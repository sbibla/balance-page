# Exercise 24: Make Cards Lift on Hover

**Level:** Intermediate
**Skills practiced:** CSS transitions and hover effects

---

## Your Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude:

> Make each card gently rise up and cast a stronger shadow when you hover your mouse over it. It should feel like the card is floating up off the page. The movement should be smooth, not sudden.

---

## What to look for when done

- Moving your mouse over a card makes it visibly lift
- The shadow underneath gets bigger and darker
- The movement is smooth (not a jump)
- Moving away returns it to normal
