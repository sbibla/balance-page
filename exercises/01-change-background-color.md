# Exercise 1: Change the Background Color

**Level:** Beginner
**Skills practiced:** Editing a CSS color value

---

## Your Prompt

Copy and paste this into Claude:

> Change the background color of the webpage to a light green color.

---

## What to look for when done

- The page background is now green instead of blue-gray or purple
- The header and cards still look normal
- The change happened in style.css
